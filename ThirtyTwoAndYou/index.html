About Us

We're here to educate people on Basic First Aid and Life Support!

How it started

In 2006 my younger brother suffered a fatal heart attack, he was only 32 years old. Knowing he was in cardiac arrest, a bystander grabbed an AED off the wall. Not having been trained in CPR/AED, no compressions were given. When the EMTs arrived, they found the AED on the floor next to him. It was never opened, no one knew how to use it. Who we are

We are NYS certified EMT-Bs, that facilitate American Heart Association (AHA) HeartSaver and BLS level CPR/AED and First Aid certifications. Whether you are interested in learning at the bystander or the basic life support levels, learning for the first time or need to recertify, 30:2 and You offers a range of classes to meet your needs.
